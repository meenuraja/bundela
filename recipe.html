Here's Your All Dishes For You 🫕

Sweets

  • 🍩Gulab Jamun

    Ingredients: Khoya, Sugar, Cardamom

    Instructions: Fry khoya balls and dip in sugar syrup.

  • 💧Rasgulla

    Ingredients: Chhena, Sugar, Water

    Instructions: Boil chhena balls in sugar syrup.

  • 🍪besan laddu

    Ingredients: Ingredients: Besan, ghee, sugar, cardamom.

    Instructions:Roast besan in ghee, cool slightly, mix sugar & elaichi, roll into laddoos.

  • 🍫mava burfi

    Ingredients: Mava (khoya), sugar, cardamom, pistachios.

    Instructions: Cook mava on low flame till soft. Add sugar and cardamom, stir till thick. Set in a plate, garnish with pistachios, cool and cut into pieces

  • 💎kaju katli

    Ingredients: Cashews (kaju), sugar, water, cardamom (optional).

    Instructions: Fry khoya balls and dip in sugar syrup.

  • 🔸jalebi

    Ingredients: Maida (all-purpose flour), curd, sugar, water, saffron, ghee/oil.

    Instructions: Make smooth batter with maida and curd. Ferment it, pipe into hot ghee in spiral shape. Fry till crisp, dip in warm sugar syrup.

  • 🟤peda

    Ingredients: Mawa (khoya), sugar, cardamom powder, ghee, saffron (optional).

    Instructions: Cook mawa in ghee till soft. Add sugar and cardamom, mix well. Cool slightly, shape into small pedas

  • 🪔modak

    Ingredients: Rice flour, jaggery, coconut, cardamom.

    Instructions: Make filling with coconut & jaggery. Prepare rice flour dough. Fill, shape into modak, and steam till soft

  • 🍥cham-cham

    Ingredients: Chhena, sugar, water, cardamom, dry fruits.

    Instructions: Shape chhena into ovals, boil in sugar syrup, cool, garnish with dry fruits or cream..

  • 🥛rabri

    Ingredients: Full cream milk, sugar, cardamom, dry fruits.

    Instructions:Boil milk, reduce to thick consistency. Add sugar, cardamom, and dry fruits. Chill and serve.

  • 🍮sooji halwa

    Ingredients: Sooji, ghee, sugar, water, cardamom, dry fruits.

    Instructions: Roast sooji in ghee. Add hot water and sugar. Cook till thick. Garnish with dry fruits.

  • 🥕gajar halwa

    Ingredients:Grated carrot, milk, sugar, ghee, dry fruits.

    Instructions: Cook carrots in milk till soft. Add sugar, ghee, and dry fruits. Stir till thick and glossy..

  • 🍯malpua

    Ingredients: Maida, milk, sugar, cardamom, ghee.

    Instructions: Make batter with maida & milk. Fry small pancakes in ghee. Dip in sugar syrup. Serve warm.

  • 🥄rasmalai

    Ingredients: Chhena balls, milk, sugar, cardamom, saffron.

    Instructions:Boil chhena balls in sugar syrup. Soak in thickened milk flavored with cardamom and saffron.

Juices

  • 🥭 Mango Juice

    Ingredients: Ripe mangoes, sugar, water, ice cubes.

    Instructions: Blend mango pulp with water and sugar. Serve chilled with ice.

  • 🍊 Orange Juice

    Ingredients: Fresh oranges, sugar (optional), ice.

    Instructions: Squeeze oranges or blend, strain, add sugar if needed. Serve cold.

  • 🍉 Watermelon Juice

    Ingredients: Watermelon cubes, mint, lemon juice.

    Instructions: Blend watermelon with mint and lemon. Strain and serve chilled.

  • 🍍 Pineapple Juice

    Ingredients: Pineapple chunks, sugar, water, ice.

    Instructions: Blend pineapple with water and sugar. Serve with ice.

  • 🍎 Apple Juice

    Ingredients: Apples, sugar, lemon juice, water.

    Instructions: Blend apples with water and lemon. Strain and serve chilled.

  • 🍷 Pomegranate Juice

    Ingredients: Pomegranate seeds, sugar (optional).

    Instructions: Blend and strain seeds. Add sugar if desired. Serve cold.

  • 🥕 Carrot Juice

    Ingredients: Carrots, ginger, lemon juice, water.

    Instructions: Blend carrots with water and ginger. Strain and add lemon juice.

  • 🍹 Mixed Fruit Juice

    Ingredients: Apple, orange, banana, pineapple, sugar, water.

    Instructions: Blend all fruits with sugar and water. Strain if needed. Serve chilled.

Achaar (Pickles)

  • 🥭 Mango Pickle

    Ingredients: Raw mangoes, mustard oil, salt, red chili, turmeric, mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds.

    Instructions: Cut mangoes, mix with spices and mustard oil. Store in jar under sun for 7–10 days.

  • 🍋 Lemon Pickle

    Ingredients: Lemons, salt, sugar, red chili, asafoetida (hing), mustard seeds.

    Instructions: Cut lemons, mix with salt and sugar. Let it rest. Add spices. Sun dry for a week.

  • 🌶️ Green Chilli Pickle

    Ingredients: Green chillies, mustard seeds, fennel, turmeric, mustard oil, salt.

    Instructions: Slit chillies, stuff with spice mix. Pour hot mustard oil. Store and let marinate 3–5 days.

  • 🫙 Mixed Vegetable Pickle

    Ingredients: Carrot, cauliflower, turnip, mustard oil, pickle masala.

    Instructions: Blanch veggies, dry them, then mix with oil and masala. Sun dry for 5–7 days.

  • 🍏 Amla (Gooseberry) Pickle

    Ingredients: Amla, mustard oil, fennel, mustard seeds, salt, chili, turmeric.

    Instructions: Boil or steam amla, deseed, mix with spices and oil. Keep in sunlight for 3–4 days.
